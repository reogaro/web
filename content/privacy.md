I hate being tracked across the internet just as much as you do. Therefore, this website is designed to collect the absolute minimum amount of data required to physically transmit these pages to your screen.

Here is exactly what happens when you visit:

### 1. No Tracking, No Cookies, No Analytics
This website uses **zero tracking cookies**. There is no Google Analytics, no Meta Pixel, no cross-site profiling, and no advertising trackers of any kind. I don't know who you are, and I want to keep it that way.

### 2. Hosting & Security (Cloudflare)
This site is statically hosted on Cloudflare Pages. As is technically required for the internet to function, Cloudflare briefly sees and logs your IP address when you request a page. This data is used exclusively to prevent DDoS attacks and route traffic efficiently. I do not have access to these logs, nor do I analyze them.

Furthermore, this server deploys a draconian **Content Security Policy (CSP)**. The server cryptographically forbids your browser from executing any third-party scripts, connecting to external ad networks, or loading remote tracking pixels. It is physically impossible for a third-party tracker to run on this domain.

### 3. Privacy-Respecting YouTube Embeds
Sometimes I embed my own YouTube videos on this blog. To protect your privacy, these embeds are locked behind a custom facade.
When you load a page with a video, **your browser does not contact Google or YouTube**. 
It is only if you explicitly click the "Play" button that an iframe is loaded from `youtube-nocookie.com` (Google's official privacy-enhanced domain). Clicking play signifies your consent to load data from YouTube's servers.

### 4. Contact
If you send me an email, I will obviously see your email address and whatever you wrote to me. I only use it to reply to you, and I don't share your email with anyone else.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, feel free to reach out via the contact link in the footer.
